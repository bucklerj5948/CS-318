THE MODERN MUSIC SCENE

The modern music scene can be extremely difficult to truly decipher and understand, especially if you're only used to, or familiar with, the basic primitive music genres//categories. Music, since then, has burst into a fantastic explosion of evolution, inspiration, and innovation; artists across the world are pioneering their own sounds, and as time carries on, the music (somehow) keeps getting even more and more interesting, intriguing, and addicting to the human ear.

Music has been a staple in culture all across the world, and the normalization of creating music electronically has impacted the music scene in fascinating ways; the ability to make music electronically in todays day and age is incredibly easy and accessible, and isn't too pricey of an endeavor at all. Due to how easily accessible producing music is nowadays, the amount of individuals getting into producing is colossal, and keeps on growing. With this growth comes evolution and innovation within the type of music that is being produced. People are completely changing the way certain music genres are percieved and utilized; artists are mixing electronica with pop music, mixing hip-hop with grunge alternative rock music, r&b//soul with house music, and the list goes on and on and on! It's truly fascinating and wonderful to see the sporadic direction that certain music is stretching towards. It feels like every year, music and music genres reach new heights that no one really thought possible, or really just thought could potentially even exist.

Within this mini-website, you will learn about a variety of different music genres that are taking over the modern music scene, as well as certain artists and producers who helped pioneer the genres, including those who make music of that specific genre, that have since then made a name for themselves within that genre, propelling it to new heights and offering new light and exposure to that genre.
